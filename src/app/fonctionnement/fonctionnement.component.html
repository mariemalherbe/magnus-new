How it works ?

Since we are very nice, we let you know some secrets about Magnus !

  • No signal

    With magnus you need no Wifi, no Bluetooth, no 4G… And find your way in big indoor spaces !

  • Geomagnetisme

    Our solution uses geomagnetisme to locate you without using any signal. It’s safe and economic !

  • Collect

    The Magnus API collects and analyzes environmental data to locate you inside a building. This data can be also matched with strategically positioned Bluetooth beacons for higher precision.
